About Us

Engr. Sahib Khan

Administrator Excellence Academy

Engr. Sahib Khan is a highly qualified and experienced professional. He holds a B.E. in Electronics and an M.E. in Energy Systems from Sukkur IBA University. He has worked as a Research Associate at Sukkur IBA for 3 years and served as a visiting faculty member at both the main campus and Kandhkot campus for 2 years.

Engr. Khan was also part of a fully funded joint research project with the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in China, where he spent 15 days conducting research. His academic contributions include 8 research papers published in international journals and conferences.

With his strong technical background, he is currently working in the Education Department, Government of Sindh. He is also the owner and administrator of Excellence Academy Sita Road.
